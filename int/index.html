We are kshetra Consulting

Kshetra is a Telangana,Karnataka, and Maharashtra-based non-profit political consulting organization that has spent the last 7 years working tp strengthen Indian democracy.Initiated by Rishikesh Reddy an XLRI Alumini and later co-founded by alumini of topIndian institutes(IIT-Madras,Delhi and IIM-A),Kshetra provides non-partism support ranging from grassroots mobilization to policy design to help political parties, leaders, groups and change-makers across the ideological spectrum

Director's Message

Kshetra is an organization focused to aid politicians in their research, program management, campaign operations, CSR fund allocation, development meetings, focused block development programs. We are a group of young professionals contributing to the public policy and political space with a vision to create an impact beyond electioneering in the larger sociopolitical realm. As we pursue our vision, we aim to partner with the development-oriented leaders and political parties of India.

Our Mission

At Kshetra Consulting, we are dedicated to strengthening Indian democracy through expert political consulting, grassroots support, and cutting-edge research. Our non-partisan approach ensures that we work towards the betterment of the entire political landscape.

About Kshetra Consulting

Our Story

Kshetra Consulting was born out of a vision to transform the political landscape of India. Founded by a group of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, we set out to bridge the gap between political aspirations and effective governance.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to provide data-driven, non-partisan support to political entities, enabling them to make informed decisions that benefit the citizens of India.

Our History

  • 2017

    Kshetra Consulting founded in Hyderabad

  • 2019

    Expanded operations to Karnataka and Maharashtra

  • 2021

    Launched the Bharat Elects Fellowship program

  • 2023

    Reached the milestone of supporting 100+ political campaigns

Our Work

At Kshetra Consulting, we pride ourselves on delivering impactful results across various domains of political consulting:

  • Designed and executed successful election campaigns at local and state levels
  • Developed innovative public outreach programs for political parties
  • Provided data-driven insights to shape political strategies
  • Organized grassroots mobilization efforts across diverse communities
